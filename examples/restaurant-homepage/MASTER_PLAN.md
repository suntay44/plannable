# MASTER_PLAN.md

## Product Goal
Build a Restaurant Homepage for presenting a restaurant brand and fast ordering actions.

## Product Scenarios

### SCN-001: First-time visitor understands the brand
Outcome: Homepage clearly communicates cuisine, location, hours, and atmosphere.

### SCN-002: Returning user can order quickly
Outcome: Returning users can quickly reach the order path.

### SCN-003: Mobile visitor browses food visually
Outcome: Mobile visitors can inspect food imagery, names, and key ordering details without layout friction.
